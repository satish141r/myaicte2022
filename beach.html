Gokarna Beach.

Wikipedia Directions

One of the most popular beaches in Gokarna, Gokarna Beach recently gained fame for being a surfing site. It is also famous for its scenic trekking excursion one can take from here.

Located in close proximity to the Mahabaleshwara Temple, the Gokarna Beach is also known as the place where pilgrims first come to take a dip before visiting the temple. 

This beach gives off some of the most beautiful sights of the ocean and the surrounding hillocks. Some of the adventure activities you can engage in Gokarna Beach include surfing, jet skiing, snorkeling as well as trekking.

Location: Gorkana Village | Ankola, Gokarna, India.

Best Time to visit: October to March.

Activities and Watersports: Yoga, Surfing, Jet ski, Banana boat, Snorkeling, and Trekking.

Distance from Gokarna Bus Main Stand: Around a KM away.

OM Beach.

Wikipedia Directions

The beach is shaped in a way that it appears to be the auspicious symbol 'OM' in the Devanagari script and is named after it. Om beach is one of the pristine and happening beaches in Gokarna for the plethora of water sports and activities that people cherish here. From the bumpy banana ride to the speedy jet ski ride, from beachside trekking on the hilly terrain to spotting Dolphin in the middle of the sea; OM beach is spot on. The beach can be enjoyed between the two coves that make the shape of OM and is loaded with shacks, huts, and cafes for a lazy vacation.

Location: Om Beach, Gokarna, Karnataka 581326.

Best Time to visit: November to March.

Activities and Water Sports:Jet ski, Trekking, Trekking, Swimming.

Distance from Gokarna Bus Main Stand: Around 6 KM.

Paradise Beach

Directions

Your visit to Puducherry won’t be complete if you didn’t take some time out for Paradise Beach. Also known as Plage Paradiso, Paradise Beach, widely famous for its unparalleled charm, mesmerizing beauty, and jaw-dropping scenery, offers a peaceful yet delightful experience to all those who are looking to relax and rejuvenate in serenity. A preferable destination for holidaymakers of all ages, Paradise Beach is one of its own kind of beach in Puducherry that invites tourists to enjoy a laid-back atmosphere along with witnessing incredible views that are hard to find anywhere else. Located just a short stroll away from Chunnambar town, this beach allows you to admire its thick mangrove trees, glistening waters, and extraordinary soft sands that enhance its overall beauty and make it look different from other popular beaches within the city. Here you can lay down under the shade of the sun, walk on the edge of its coastline, play your favorite water sport, or simply choose to satisfy your hunger at nearby eateries after an adventurous day. All-in-all, it won’t be wrong to say that Paradise Beach has everything you need to make the most of your trip and is definitely a great place to spend some unforgettable time with your loved ones.

Things To Do at Paradise Beach, Puducherry

  • Play Your Favourite Beach Sports

    • Try your hand at your favorite beach sport from football, volleyball, cricket, or badminton to bring plenty of memories back home. The soft golden sand of this beach makes it an ideal location for outdoor sports lovers to engage in beach sports. You can either choose to bring your own ball from home or simply ask the beach authorities for one.

  • Indulge in Water Adventures

    • Choose from a diverse range of adventurous activities such as Canoeing, Kayaking, and Jet Skiing to explore the adventurous side in you. Please note that the beach is quite deep, so it is important to take all safety precautions before starting any activity. Also, these activities are not designed to be available 365 days on the beach. Hence, check with the authorities first before heading to this beach in case you are coming here for adventure activities only

  • Photography

    • Haven for photographers, Paradise Beach offers innumerable opportunities to capture some amazing photographs that you’ll cherish forever. It has attractions beyond your imagination. Here you can take pictures of a beautiful landscape along with many inspiring sights that look stunning when clicked throughout the boat ride.

  • Walking

    • How about enjoying a leisure walk with your partner in the evening? Yes, it’s indeed the nicest thing to do every time you are on this beach. All you need is to take out your sandals or shoes and enjoy a short stroll with bare feet along the length of the beach. And when you get tired, you can sit on the soft sand and experience some ME time in privacy

  • Eating

    • Treat your taste buds with a wide variety of mouthwatering dishes served at the beach shacks. The local vendors at Paradise Beach sell some hot bhajjis that are a must-try. You can also order from a large assortment of beverages to complement your meal. Don’t forget to sip the fresh and tender coconut water here. In addition to these, there are several other things that you can do at Paradise Beach. These include fishing, boating, swimming, watching the sunrise, etc

Half Moon Beach

Directions

The lovely Half-Moon beach is the third beach in Gokarna's coastal belt. The beach is roughly 63.8 kilometres from Karwar and only a few kilometres from Gokarna's major city. It is separated from Om Beach by a hill from where visitors may enjoy a spectacular view of the sea. Several village cottages in the typical Indian architecture can be seen lined up along the shore, giving it a pretty traditional and cosy feel. The gorgeous beach is ideal for unwinding and chilling out amid the big blue seas and the soft sand, staring at the caverns and the star-lit sky.

Why must you visit Half Moon Beach?

Swimming in the cool waters of the Half Moon beach is among the most exhilarating feelings. All you can see ahead of you is the boundless ocean and behind you is the dense forest, making you feel like you're the last surviving person on the planet. Diving, kayaking, paddling boaters, paintball and camping are just a few of the wonderful activities available. The cuisine served in the shack eateries is delicious to add more to the list. Half Moon Beach, which overlooks the whole coastline of Om Beach, is undoubtedly a vacation place for wanderlusts looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and waiting to dive into nature's lap. Water activities, on the other hand, are not available. Lying on the beach in the daytime and laying in hammocks at night are other ways for visitors to relax.

Trekking to the Half Moon Beach

The journey to Half-Moon beach starts at Om Beach. First, proceed to the southern end of Om Beach and up the small route that runs straight to the Dolphin Café. A whiteboard outside the café marks the spot of the Half Moon beach and points in its direction. You can spot a hiking trail junction a few minutes into the hike. You will see woody and prickly shrubs along this small trekking trail along the coast. On the left-hand side of the route are hills, and on the right-hand side is a beautiful blue sea. The winding trekking trail leads you to a five-minute hike through the forest. It will take you around four to five minutes to walk around the hillside and a few more minutes till you reach the Half Moon beach.

Small hell beach

Directions

The stunning Small Hell beach is a must-visit tourist spot in Gokarna. The natural backdrop and the gorgeous sealine make the beach a picturesque location for photography lovers. The golden sand and bright blue water of the Arabian sea add to the beauty of the beach. Small rocks and cliffs on one side and the gorgeous green tree cover on the other make your experience worthwhile. A small yet famous Hell cliff is situated here that gives an expansive view of the coastline and a chance to enjoy the gorgeous sunrise and sunset. It is one of the cleanest beaches in Gokarna and has a few cafes and hotels along the shore. Shaiva tent cafe and Kahani Paradise are good places to spend a night.

Location of the Small Hell beach

The Small Hell beach is situated on the Gokarna main Road.

Entry fee and timings of Small Hell beach

There is no entry fee to the Small Hell beach. It is open for visitors all through the year.

Best time to visit the Small Hell beach

You will see a crowd of tourists in Gokarna between October to March as the weather conditions are perfect for enjoying the beaches and temples. The water is calm, and the views are breathtaking. It is advisable to avoid the summer season from April to May as the weather gets really hot and humid. The Konkan coast experiences extreme rainfall between June and September, leaving the place wet and murky and not ideal for exploring.

How to reach the Small Hell beach?

There is no second thought about how beautiful the route to Gokarna can be. Whether it is your bus journey or the road trip from a nearby city in Karnataka, the route to Gokarna will leave you with some amazing memories. The Small Hell beach is just 7.5 km from the Gokarna main bus stand and you can book a private cab from top car rental companies in Gokarna to reach the beach. While it is well-connected by rail, road, and air from different cities around Gokarna, you must find the availability before booking your tickets. The nearest airport to Gokarna is the Dabolim airport in Goa, approx. 135 kms and will take you 3 hours to reach. The nearest railway station is in Gokarna, Kumte, and Ankola, which lie short distances from each other. Quite a few trains have a brief stop here before proceeding further. If you are an adventure lover, then the Small Hell beach is the perfect tourist destination in Karnataka.
